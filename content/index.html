Rosa SanMarchi is a Chicago based actor. She's a member of SAG-AFTRA and is represented by Grossman & Jack Talent.

Prior to moving Chicago, she acted in and produced numerous on-camera and theatrical projects in both her hometown of Phoenix and in Los Angeles. This past summer Rosa graduated from the 2015 School at Steppenwolf.

Rosa is thrilled to be making her Steppenwolf Theatre Company debut in Domesticated, written and directed by Pulitzer Prize-winning author Bruce Norris.

Domesticated runs December 13, 2015 - February 7, 2016.
